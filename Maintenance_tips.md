# Home Maintenance Tips

## General Tips
- Regularly check your HVAC filters.
- Clean gutters at least twice a year.
- Inspect the roof for damage after storms.

## Seasonal Tips
- Winterize your home before the first freeze.
- Check and clean the chimney before winter.
- Inspect and seal windows and doors to improve insulation.

## Safety Tips
- Test smoke detectors and replace batteries annually.
- Keep a fire extinguisher accessible in the kitchen.
- Install carbon monoxide detectors near bedrooms.
